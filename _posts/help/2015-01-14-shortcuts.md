`2` Focus Omnibar

`F` Navigate to Following stream

`N` Navigate to Starred stream

`R` Navigate to Notifications

`S` Navigate to Search

`D` Navigate to Discover

`=` Toggle grid mode for main content

`?` Show help modal

`CMD/CTRL + Enter` Submit a post or comment.
